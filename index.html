ABOUT ME

Hello everyone, I am a Designer

I have been doing 3D graphics, design and visualization for over 17 years. I have realized more than 100 big projects (including local and international level) and a lot of various projects in different directions. I started my ascent in 2005 by designing and visualizing interior solutions. My specialties are 3D modeling, Game Development, shaders making, visualization, generative design, image analysis and Image processing, reproduction, compositing, light, color and everything connected with it. Also my area of interest includes: IT, hardware, backend development, Distributed systems, System Administration, Data Storage, Networking/Cyber Security, WEB technology and layout, electronics, power generation, lighting and embedded systems.

For a long time I was a leading designer in large companies, I prefer teamwork, when everyone understands what he is doing and can rely on all members of the project.
